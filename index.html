Automotive Restorations


At DaBodyChop we build your vehicle the way you’ve always dreamed of. We restore each vehicle as if it were our own with a passion that has created some of the finest show-winning vehicles found anywhere.


Driver Restorations


If you plan on driving your vehicle some what guilt-free, you may not want it to be perfect in all respects. Vehicles restored at this level stand out in shows as high quality vehicles but resources spent on certain areas would be minimized depending on your specific goals and expectations.



Progressive Restorations



For those of you already enjoying a collector vehicle, and those years are starting to show, we offer our progressive restoration service. With this approach we set per-visit milestones for your specific restoration allowing you to maximize the amount of time you can enjoy your vehicle. All steps that are taken follow MCR’s standard tried-and-tested procedures to ensure the highest quality.
